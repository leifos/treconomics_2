News Search System Study

This HIT involves performing four search tasks of approx. 10 minutes each using a Newspaper based search engine.

For each search task, you will be given a topic, such as:

Find and bookmark articles that discuss instances of piracy, where DIFFERENT BOATS/SHIPS have been illegally taken control of or boarded.
After each search task, you will be asked to recall what BOATS/SHIPS you found that were involved in acts of piracy --- followed by a series of short survey questions asking you about your search experience and the search system.

The HIT will take approximately 80 minutes.

Important Notes

  • All the collected data will be treated as confidential and it will not be possible to identify you as a person with the data after the experiment as ended.
  • You should be at least 18 years old.
  • This study must be completed in a single sitting.
  • Do not exit the process unless you intend to decline the HIT.
  • You can only participate in this study once.
  • In order to conduct this experiment you need to use Safari, Firefox or Chrome, only. If you use any other browser, your HIT may not be accepted.
  • Your screen resolution should be at least 1024x720 or above, otherwise your HIT will not be accepted.
  • The study will appear in a pop-up so you will need to enable/allow the popup in your browser.
  • You must complete all search tasks successfully to be paid.
  • After the demographics survey you will have the chance to undertake a short practice task, where you can decide to continue or not.
